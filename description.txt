TerraSouls brings a souls-like experience to Terraria:

- Leveling system with Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, and Faith.
- Weapons with stat scaling.
- Bloodstains upon death to restore souls and humanity.
- Elemental resistances and weaknesses on enemies and bosses.
- New rings, items, recipes, and unique drops.
- Revamped death screen and start menu.

More items, bosses, and mechanics coming soon!

Visit the repository on GitHub (https://github.com/Jcarrasco96/dark-souls-terraria) and join the community.
